About me

Who
I am

I work where creative direction meets hands-on making. I develop social-first ideas, formats, and campaigns with a sharp eye for how content moves, lands, and resonates across platforms.

I'm a strategic thinker and a creative maker. I turn ideas and strategy into digital worlds people actually want to be part of. With a background in campaigning and social media strategy, I build stories, visuals, and formats that connect brands and audiences in meaningful ways.

My strength? Turning trends, cultural signals, and audience behaviour into content that cuts through the noise. I find that sweet spot where creativity meets strategy, where content doesn't just look good but feels like something worth engaging with. Because concept is everything.

I focus on building creative directions and formats that can scale, evolve, and keep audiences coming back. And I thrive in collaborative environments, working closely with creators, strategists, and stakeholders to raise the bar and bring ideas to life.

SlukSammen Campaign Hero

With this campaign and our SlukSammen ambassadors, we challenged Denmark to put down the phone. The campaign ran across outdoor placements, cinemas, and Netto store facades. It generated significant press coverage, including features on TV2 Kosmopol and TV2 NEWS. We also partnered with Frederik Næblerød and Farshad Farzankia on two limited-edition t-shirt prints, each representing their personal take on living offline or being dependent on your phone. The campaign reached over 1.5 million people on social media and sparked a grassroots wave of initiatives, with everyday people organizing their own phone-free events. I served as project manager for the entire campaign, created the social content, and kept the momentum going across all the different initiatives. The campaign continued the following year. Campaign film by Elvin Micimovic — concept, edit, and script.

Det Okay Campaign

"Det' Okay" shines a light on vulnerability in all its forms. The campaign brings mental health struggles out of the shadows and in front of the camera, where photographer Petra Kleis beautifully captures the strengths and vulnerabilities of our headspace ambassadors through intimate portraits. The campaign also extended into listeners' ears through the "Det' Okay" podcast, which reached #1 on Apple Podcasts and Spotify and generated significant press coverage. I developed the original concept, led the campaign as project manager, shaped the visual identity, and handled the PR strategy.

TikTok
Nomi&Loke × Rasmus Søndergaard × headspace

In collaboration with the duo Nomi&Loke, we put the spotlight on boys and the importance of talking about feelings. In a down-to-earth way, staying true to Nomi and Loke's own tone and approach, we launched a video podcast series where they explore men's mental health and their relationship with emotions. The primary goal was to build a genuine connection with young boys as a target audience, making headspace feel more approachable and easier to reach out to for help. The series ran as advent episodes, with a new guest dropping every Sunday throughout advent. The TikTok videos delivered strong organic results, ranging from 20K to 100K views per episode. I was the driving force behind the concept development, served as campaign project manager, and managed the ongoing dialogue with the creators around content, format, and revisions.

DeleForældre Campaign Film

Many children move between parents every seven days because of divorce. With this campaign film, we flipped the script and showed, in a comedic way, what it would look like if the parents were the ones living as "divorce kids." I came up with the idea and wrote the script. The concept grew out of my research with our in-house specialists, one of whom noted: "It's not the parents who have to move back and forth in a shared custody arrangement." Directed by Elvin Micimovic — shoot and edit.
